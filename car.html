FAQ

What are your rates for hourly rental?

Our standard rate for hourly rental is ₹250 per hour with a minimum of 4 hours booking. For 8+ hours, we offer discounted packages.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

For local rentals, fuel (CNG/Petrol) is included. For outstation trips, the car will be provided with a full tank and should be returned with a full tank.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours before the booking time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a charge of 50% of the booking amount.

Do you provide drivers?

Yes, all our rentals come with experienced, verified drivers at no extra cost. The driver's food and accommodation during outstation trips are to be taken care of by the customer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and cash. A 20% advance is required to confirm bookings.
